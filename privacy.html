This information is used to operate and secure the website, understand website usage, diagnose technical problems, and improve content and user experience.

Google Analytics

Tweaknook uses Google Analytics to understand how visitors use the website. Google Analytics may use cookies, including the _ga cookie, and similar technologies to collect information such as page views, session activity, device and browser information, approximate geographic location, and referring pages.

Google processes this information on our behalf according to its own terms and privacy practices. You can learn more by visiting Google's Privacy Policy. You may also use the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Cloudflare

Tweaknook uses Cloudflare for website delivery, performance, and security. Cloudflare may process technical information such as IP addresses, request headers, requested URLs, timestamps, network information, and security-related data when visitors access the website.

Cloudflare processes this information to deliver website content, prevent abuse, and protect the website. For more information, review Cloudflare's Privacy Policy.

Cookies and Similar Technologies

Cookies are small text files stored on a visitor's device. Tweaknook and third-party services may use cookies or similar technologies for website operation, analytics, security, remembering choices, measuring advertising performance, and displaying advertisements.

You can block or delete cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some website features.
